📖 README_tools.md Updates
​Footer Multi-line: The footer.toml now supports two lines. 
The script automatically stacks them and centers them.
​Asset Swapping: To change themes, just swap header.toml or
footer.toml with one of your samples from the /Template directory and re-run the script.
​Pentoo Specifics: For XFCE Stage 4 environments, 
ensure media-gfx/imagemagick is compiled with png and jpeg support in your make.conf.
